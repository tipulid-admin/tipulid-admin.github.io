Sleep Without Sheep™

Available soon in the App Store

Quiets your mind from recurring thoughts

The Sleep Without Sheep™ app is designed to help you sleep and relax. As the name implies, it is based on the idea of counting sheep, but much improved, and without sheep.

A different sequence every time

A set of pleasant and rich imaginations help lead your mind away from other thoughts or stresses of the current day. The sequence of imaginations will always be different, and you can also choose from different themes for the imagary.

Eyes Closed Control™ for full relaxation

Want to adjust or restart the app while you are trying to fall asleep or wake up in the middle of the night? Sleep Without Sheep™ provides several ways of controlling essential functions of the app with your eyes closed.
