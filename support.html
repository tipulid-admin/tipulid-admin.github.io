To help you fall asleep make a mental picture of each animal as it’s voiced. We’ve given the full name of each animal, but mostly for the rhythm of the words, and few people will know the difference between a reticulated giraffe and an African giraffe, so just imagine to whatever level of specificity that is relaxing to you, and if you don’t know the animal at all, just imagine what you think it might be.

Sleep well!
